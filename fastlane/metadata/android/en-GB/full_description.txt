<b>Note: MyAnimeList is now back online, but their API is still unavailable, as such this application will only work with Kitsu until MyAnimeList is back online.</b>

App to interface with Kitsu & MyAnimeList to keep your current anime and manga series progress up to date.

The app will let you: 
* Add a new anime/manga series to track 
* Update current series episode/chapter number 
* Change series status 

This app does NOT allow you to read or watch anything, this merely allows you to track your current progress. 

Note: A free account with Kitsu or MyAnimeList is required to use this application.
